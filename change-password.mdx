For your account's security, you can update your password anytime through the **Settings** page. Here's how to change your password:

![Changing your password](/images/password.jpg)

## How to Change Your Password

1. **Access the Settings Page**:
   - Navigate to the **Sidebar Menu** and select **Settings**.
   - Alternatively, click your **Username** at the bottom-left corner of the sidebar, then choose **Settings** from the dropdown menu.

2. **Locate the Password Section**:
   - On the **Settings** page, scroll down to the section underneath **Account Name** labeled **Change Password**.

3. **Update Your Password**:
   - Enter your **current password** in the first field.
   - Enter your **new password** in the second field.
   - Confirm your new password by typing it again in the third field.

4. **Save Changes**:
   - Click the **Update Password** button to apply your changes.

## Additional Notes

- Choose a strong password that includes a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters.
- If you forget your current password, use the **Forgot Password** option on the login page to reset it.
- Your password change will take effect immediately after updating.

Keep your account secure by regularly updating your password!
