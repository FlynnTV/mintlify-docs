If you're having trouble logging in or need to reset your password, follow these steps to regain access to your account:

![Forgot password page](/images/resetpassword.jpg)

## How to Reset Your Password

1. **Use the Forgot Password Option**:
   - On the **Login Screen**, click the **Forgot Password** link.

2. **Follow the Reset Instructions**:
   - You will be prompted to enter your email address associated with your account.
   - Check your inbox for a password reset email and follow the instructions provided.

3. **Alternative: Use the Direct Reset Link**:
   - You can also reset your password by following this [Reset Password Link](https://flynnsai.com/login?state=reset).

4. **Need Help?**:
   - If you're still experiencing issues, feel free to contact our support team at **hello@flynnsai.com**.
