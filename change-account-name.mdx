Updating your account name is simple and can be done through the **Settings** page. Follow the steps below to change your username.

## How to Change Your Account Name

![Update account name](/images/username.jpg)

1. **Access the Settings Page**:
   - Navigate to the **Sidebar Menu** and select **Settings**.
   - Alternatively, click your **Username** at the bottom-left corner of the sidebar, then select **Settings** from the dropdown menu.

2. **Locate the Username Option**:
   - On the **Settings** page, find the option labeled **Change Username**.

3. **Update Your Username**:
   - Enter your new desired username in the provided field.

4. **Save Changes**:
   - Click the **Update** button to save your new username.

## Additional Notes

- Ensure your new username meets any requirements or restrictions (e.g., length or special characters).
- The change will take effect immediately after updating.
- You can revisit the **Settings** page at any time to make further updates.

Enjoy the flexibility to personalize your account with your preferred username!
